Bio

Backstreet Boys (often abbreviated as BSB) are an American vocal group, formed in Orlando, Florida in 1993. The group rose to fame with their debut international album, Backstreet Boys (1996). The Backstreet Boys have sold over 100 million records worldwide, making them the best-selling boy bank of all time, and one of the world's best-selling music artists.
